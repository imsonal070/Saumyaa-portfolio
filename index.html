About Saumyaa

Hello! I'm Saumya Jha, a 22-year-old professional model and PR based in Delhi, currently pursuing my B.Com (3rd year). With over 2 years of industry experience, I've worked across diverse projects ranging from traditional to western and Indo-western styles, both in studio and on outdoor locations

Standing at 5'6" (without heels) with a well-balanced physique (32-28/30-34), I bring elegance and confidence to every frame. My medium to fair skin tone, black eyes, and brown hair offer a versatile look for varied concepts and campaigns. I’m fluent in Hindi and English, comfortable with all shooting schedules, and open to outstation as well as international shoots (currently without a passport).

I'm passionate about fashion and self-expression, always striving to bring authenticity and professionalism to every shoot. While I'm open to creative and impactful storytelling, I maintain clear boundaries regarding on-screen intimacy and bold scenes. I’m not comfortable with bikini shoots, smooch/kiss scenes, or bold content.

Whether it’s Indian wear, western outfits, skirts, or shorts, I adapt seamlessly to the theme and bring my full energy to the camera. I have no allergies to cosmetics or dust, making me flexible for both indoor and outdoor environments.

If you're looking for a committed, expressive, and experienced model who respects creativity with clarity—I'd love to collaborate!
